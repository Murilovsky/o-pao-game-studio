Mind The Gap

It's a 2D side-scrolling game, with an high emphasis on exploration and puzzle- solving.Those mysteries, a staple of the horror genre, are a pivotal part of our game. From the very beginning the the bitter end, the main character George is surrounded by mysteries and eerie happenings, that will keep the player on the edge of their seats.

The story revolves around figuring out what happened to the main character, while simultaneously referencing the stages of grief throuought the game. Every stage will represent different feelings, both in art and in gameplay, making the player empathize with George's feelings
